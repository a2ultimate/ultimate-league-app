Player Code of Conduct

As a player I agree to the following:

  1. I will uphold the spirit of the game and treat others with respect.
  2. I will never engage in physical violence against another player for any reason (including retaliation).
  3. I will never enact verbal abuse against another player for any reason (e.g. yelling, swearing).
  4. I will never play in such a way that endangers or frightens other players (e.g. overly aggressive play or dangerous bids).

Additionally, I understand and agree to the following:

  1. Registering does not guarantee me the right to play; captains and/or league officials may remove a player from play at any time for a violation of this Code of Conduct.
  2. Failing to comply with this Code of Conduct could result in my suspension from the league, possibly permanently, per the jurisdiction of the Ann Arbor Ultimate Board of Directors.
